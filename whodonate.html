Who Can Donate Blood

You can give blood if you:

    Are fit and healthy
    Weigh over 50kg
    Are aged between 17 and 66 years (or 70 if you have given blood before)
    Are over 70 years and have given blood in the last 2 years
    Have haemoglobin level of not less than 12.0 g/dl for females and not less than 13.0 g/dl for males as the threshold.

How Often Can I Give Blood

    Men can give every 12 weeks
    Women can give every 16 weeks
